Life Expectancy

Life expectancy is a statistical measure that represents the average number of years a person can expect to live, based on the current mortality rates and other demographic factors.

Factors Influencing Life Expectancy

Several factors contribute to life expectancy, including:

  • Access to quality healthcare
  • Nutrition and diet
  • Socioeconomic conditions
  • Public health interventions
  • Education and literacy
  • Sanitation and hygiene
  • Genetic factors

Importance of Life Expectancy

Life expectancy serves as a vital indicator of the overall health and well-being of a population. It helps in assessing the progress in healthcare systems, identifying areas for improvement, and informing policy decisions related to healthcare, social security, and retirement planning.
